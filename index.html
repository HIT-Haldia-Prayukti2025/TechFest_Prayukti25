Overview

Prayukti, HIT Haldia is the annual techno-management festival of the Haldia Institute of Technology. Prayukti originated in 2001 to provide a platform for the Indian student community to develop and showcase their technical prowess.

Prayukti serves as a national level techno-management extravaganza, bringing together students, academia, and industries on a common platform. The event offers a wide range of competitions that test participants' grit, knowledge, and skills.

Now in its 25th edition, with the theme locked in, the festival is scheduled to take place from 22nd March to 24th March. So call your friends, mark your calendars, and gear up for Prayukti 2025: Unravel the secrets of cyber world!

Read More...

Join your peers as they converge at Prayukti 2025 for a shared purpose: to learn, network, and advance their cyber security strategy. Attendees will share practical concepts and examples of rigorous security measures taken by their organizations, ways of embedding a robust culture of security consciousness, and their laser-sharp focus on the latest security initiatives and technological innovations. Be there if you want to secure, innovate, defend, and lead, all at the same time.
